Molletes recipe

This is the recipe to cook some real Mexican Molletes

Ingredients

  • 1 bolillo bread
  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of pico de gallo
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of pickled jalapeños

Steps

  1. Cut the bolillo bread in half
  2. Spread the refried beans on the bread
  3. Add the shredded cheese
  4. Put the bread in the oven until the cheese melts
  5. Top with pico de gallo, sour cream, and pickled jalapeños
